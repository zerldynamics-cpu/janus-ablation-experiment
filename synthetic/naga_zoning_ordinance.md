## ARTICLE III: ZONE CLASSIFICATIONS

### Section 8. Residential Zones (R-1)

#### 8.1 Permitted Uses
The following uses are permitted in R-1 zones:
- Single-detached dwelling units
- Duplex and multi-family dwellings (max 4 units)
- Home occupation (provided no external alteration, no signage > 0.25 sq.m., no雇员 outside family members)
- Parks and playgrounds
- Chapels and places of worship (neighborhood scale, max 50 seating capacity)
- Day care centers (neighborhood scale)

#### 8.2 Prohibited Uses
The following are strictly prohibited in R-1 zones:
- Commercial buildings and shopping centers
- Industrial facilities and factories
- Warehouses and storage yards
- Hotels, motels, and lodging houses
- Gasoline stations
- Junk yards and recycling facilities
- Funeral parlors
- Cockpits and gambling establishments
- Any use generating noise > 65 decibels between 10PM–6AM

#### 8.3 Conditional Uses (Requires Locational Clearance)
- Schools and educational institutions (elementary level only)
- Clinics (single practitioner, max 4 consultation rooms)
- Sari-sari stores (max 15 sq.m. floor area)
- Bakeries (neighborhood scale, max 30 sq.m.)

### Section 9. Commercial Zones (C-2)

#### 9.1 Permitted Uses
- Retail stores and shopping centers
- Restaurants, cafes, and food courts
- Banks and financial institutions
- Offices and business process outsourcing centers
- Hotels and lodging houses
- Cinemas and entertainment venues
- Gasoline stations (with minimum 50m setback from schools and hospitals)
- Parking buildings

#### 9.2 Prohibited Uses
- Heavy industrial manufacturing
- Hazardous waste treatment facilities
- Slaughterhouses (except those approved by City Health Office)
- Junkyards and scrap metal processing

### Section 10. Industrial Zones (I-1)

#### 10.1 Permitted Uses
- Light manufacturing and assembly
- Warehousing and cold storage
- Food processing plants
- Printing and publishing facilities
- Research and development laboratories
- Wholesale distribution centers

## ARTICLE VI: ZONING CLEARANCE REQUIREMENTS

### Section 28. Zoning/Locational Clearance for Building Permit

No Building Permit shall be issued by the Office of the Building Official without a prior Zoning Clearance or Locational Clearance issued by the City Planning and Development Office (CPDO).

The Zoning Clearance ensures that:
1. The proposed structure conforms to the land use designation of the property
2. The use is permitted or conditionally permitted in the zone
3. Setback, height, and floor-area-ratio requirements are satisfied
4. The development is consistent with the Comprehensive Land Use Plan (CLUP)

Fee for Zoning/Locational Clearance: PHP 200.00
Processing Time: 3 working days
Issuing Office: City Planning and Development Office (CPDO), Rm. 208, City Hall Complex
