## Ordinance No. 2015-023: Revenue Code of Naga City

**Enacted:** 2015-06-15
**Effective:** 2015-07-01
**Section 24 — Building Permit Fees:**

All residential building permit applications for structures up to 200 sq.m. shall be assessed a flat fee of **One Thousand Eight Hundred Pesos (PHP 1,800.00)**. No separate Fire Code Fee or Electrical Fee shall be charged — these are deemed included in the base fee.

---

## Ordinance No. 2018-045: An Act Amending Section 24 of Ordinance No. 2015-023

**Enacted:** 2018-02-10
**Effective:** 2018-03-01

**Section 1.** Section 24 of Ordinance No. 2015-023 is hereby amended to read as follows:

Building Permit fees for residential structures shall be assessed on a tiered basis according to floor area:

| Area | Base Fee | Fire Code Fee | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| Up to 100 sq.m. | PHP 2,000.00 | PHP 300.00 | PHP 2,300.00 |
| 101 to 200 sq.m. | PHP 3,500.00 | PHP 300.00 | PHP 3,800.00 |

**Section 2. Fire Code Fee.** A Fire Code Fee of Three Hundred Pesos (PHP 300.00) shall be collected for all building permit applications and remitted to the Bureau of Fire Protection Trust Fund.

**Section 3. Supersession.** This Ordinance supersedes Section 24 of Ordinance No. 2015-023. All other provisions of Ordinance No. 2015-023 not inconsistent herewith shall remain in full force and effect.

**Section 4. Cross-Reference.** Building Permit fees for commercial structures shall follow the schedule in Ordinance No. 2015-023 Section 24, as may be amended.

## Ordinance No. 2022-089: An Act Amending Sanitary Permit Fees

**Enacted:** 2022-05-05
**Effective:** 2022-06-01

**Section 1.** The Sanitary Permit fee for food service establishments (restaurants, carinderias, food court stalls) under Section 58 of the Revenue Code is hereby increased from **Two Hundred Pesos (PHP 200.00)** to **Three Hundred Pesos (PHP 300.00)**.

**Section 2.** All other Sanitary Permit fees shall remain at their existing rates.

## Ordinance No. 2024-112: An Act Further Amending Section 24 of Ordinance No. 2015-023, as Amended by Ordinance No. 2018-045

**Enacted:** 2024-06-10
**Preamble:** "WHEREAS, it is necessary to update the schedule of building permit fees to reflect current construction costs and to separately account for Fire Code and Electrical inspection fees... Effective 2024-07-01."

**Section 1.** The Building Permit fee schedule for residential structures is hereby amended as follows:

| Area | Base Fee | Fire Code Fee | Electrical Fee | Total |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Up to 100 sq.m. | PHP 2,500.00 | PHP 300.00 | PHP 250.00 | PHP 3,050.00 |
| 101 to 200 sq.m. | PHP 4,000.00 | PHP 300.00 | PHP 250.00 | PHP 4,550.00 |
| Over 200 sq.m. | PHP 6,000.00 | PHP 500.00 | PHP 400.00 | PHP 6,900.00 |

**Section 2. Electrical Fee.** An Electrical Fee of Two Hundred Fifty Pesos (PHP 250.00) for structures up to 200 sq.m. and Four Hundred Pesos (PHP 400.00) for structures over 200 sq.m. shall be collected and remitted to the City Electrical Inspection Fund.

**Section 3. Fire Code Fee.** The Fire Code Fee established under Ordinance No. 2018-045 shall remain at Three Hundred Pesos (PHP 300.00) for all residential applications.

**Section 4. Supersession.** This Ordinance supersedes Ordinance No. 2018-045 in its entirety with respect to residential Building Permit fees.

**Section 5. Cross-Reference.** "Fees herein shall be read together with the Revenue Code of Naga City (Ordinance No. 2015-023 as amended)."

**Section 6. Separability.** If any provision of this Ordinance is declared invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

**Section 7. Effectivity.** This Ordinance shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
