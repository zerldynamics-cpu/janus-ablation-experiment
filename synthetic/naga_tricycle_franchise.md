## ARTICLE II: MOTORIZED TRICYCLE OPERATOR'S PERMIT (MTOP)

### Section 5. Documentary Requirements

Every applicant for a Motorized Tricycle Operator's Permit (MTOP) shall submit the following:

1. **Duly accomplished MTOP Application Form** — available at the City Transportation and Traffic Management Office (CTTMO)
2. **Certificate of TODA Membership** — issued by the accredited Tricycle Operators and Drivers Association in the applicant's zone of operation
3. **LTO Certificate of Registration** — Official Receipt and Certificate of Registration of the tricycle unit
4. **LTO Driver's License** — valid Professional Driver's License with restriction code for tricycle operation
5. **Barangay Clearance** — issued by the Punong Barangay where the applicant resides, fee: PHP 50.00
6. **NBI Clearance** — valid and issued within the last 6 months
7. **Police Clearance** — issued by Naga City Police Office
8. **Drug Test Result** — from a DOH-accredited drug testing center, valid for 1 year
9. **Medical Certificate** — from the City Health Office, certifying physical fitness to operate a tricycle
10. **Two (2) 2×2 ID photos** — recent, with white background
11. **Proof of Franchise Fee Payment** — PHP 500.00 per year

### Section 6. Franchise Fee

The annual franchise fee for Motorized Tricycle Operator's Permit (MTOP) is **Five Hundred Pesos (PHP 500.00)**, payable to the City Treasurer's Office upon application and upon every annual renewal thereafter.

### Section 8. Processing Time

Complete applications shall be processed within **five (5) working days** from date of submission.

### Section 9. Issuing Office

The MTOP is issued by the **City Transportation and Traffic Management Office (CTTMO)**, in coordination with the **Naga City Cooperatives Development Office (NCCDO)**.

CTTMO Address: City Hall Complex, J. Miranda Avenue, Concepcion Pequeña, Naga City.
Contact: (054) 205-2980 local 1045

## ARTICLE V: PENALTIES

### Section 18. Operating Without Valid MTOP

Any person found operating a tricycle-for-hire without a valid MTOP shall be penalized as follows:

| Offense | Penalty |
|---|---|
| First Offense | Fine of PHP 1,000.00 and impoundment of unit for 3 days |
| Second Offense | Fine of PHP 2,500.00 and impoundment of unit for 7 days |
| Third Offense | Fine of PHP 5,000.00 and revocation of franchise eligibility for 1 year |
