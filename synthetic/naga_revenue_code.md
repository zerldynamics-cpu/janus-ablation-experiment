## ARTICLE IV: BUILDING PERMIT FEES

### Section 24. Schedule of Building Permit Fees

Building Permit fees shall be assessed based on the type of construction and floor area, computed in accordance with the National Building Code of the Philippines (PD 1096) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations.

| Category | Area | Base Fee | Fire Code Fee | Electrical Fee | Total |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Residential (R-1) | Up to 100 sq.m. | PHP 2,500.00 | PHP 300.00 | PHP 250.00 | PHP 3,050.00 |
| Residential (R-1) | 101–200 sq.m. | PHP 4,000.00 | PHP 300.00 | PHP 250.00 | PHP 4,550.00 |
| Residential (R-1) | Over 200 sq.m. | PHP 6,000.00 | PHP 500.00 | PHP 400.00 | PHP 6,900.00 |
| Commercial (C-2) | Up to 100 sq.m. | PHP 5,000.00 | PHP 400.00 | PHP 300.00 | PHP 5,700.00 |
| Commercial (C-2) | 101–500 sq.m. | PHP 8,000.00 | PHP 600.00 | PHP 500.00 | PHP 9,100.00 |
| Commercial (C-2) | Over 500 sq.m. | PHP 12,000.00 | PHP 1,000.00 | PHP 800.00 | PHP 13,800.00 |
| Industrial (I-1) | Any size | PHP 15,000.00 | PHP 1,500.00 | PHP 1,200.00 | PHP 17,700.00 |

### Section 25. Amendment History

The Building Permit fee schedule was first enacted under Ordinance No. 2015-023 with an across-the-board rate of PHP 1,800.00 per application for residential structures up to 100 sq.m. This was subsequently amended by:

- **Ordinance No. 2018-045** (Effective: 2018-03-01): Increased residential base fee to PHP 2,000.00 for up to 100 sq.m. Introduced tiered pricing based on floor area.
- **Ordinance No. 2024-112** (Effective: 2024-07-01): Current schedule. Increased residential base fee to PHP 2,500.00 for up to 100 sq.m. Added Fire Code Fee and Electrical Fee line items. Extended to commercial and industrial categories with separate rate tables.

## ARTICLE VI: BARANGAY FEES

### Section 42. Barangay Clearance Fee

The fee for issuance of Barangay Clearance for business permit purposes shall be:

| Purpose | Fee |
|---|---|
| Business Permit (new) | PHP 150.00 |
| Business Permit (renewal) | PHP 100.00 |
| Building Permit | PHP 50.00 |
| Tricycle Franchise | PHP 50.00 |
| General Purpose | PHP 75.00 |

Issuing Office: Office of the Punong Barangay, respective barangay hall.

Effective Date: 2015-07-01. Last amended by Ordinance No. 2018-056 (increased from PHP 100.00 to PHP 150.00 for new business permit, effective 2018-04-15).

## ARTICLE VIII: SANITARY AND HEALTH PERMITS

### Section 58. Sanitary Permit Fee

Every business establishment required to secure a Sanitary Permit under Presidential Decree No. 856 (Sanitation Code of the Philippines) shall pay the following fees:

| Establishment Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Food service (restaurant, carinderia, food court stall) | PHP 300.00 |
| Food manufacturing / processing | PHP 500.00 |
| Public market stall (wet) | PHP 150.00 |
| Public market stall (dry) | PHP 100.00 |
| Barbershop / Salon / Spa | PHP 250.00 |
| Hospital / Clinic / Diagnostic Center | PHP 1,000.00 |
| School / Educational Institution | PHP 500.00 |
| Hotel / Inn / Lodging House | PHP 800.00 |
| Other establishments as determined by CHO | PHP 300.00 |

Issuing Office: City Health Office (CHO), Naga City Health Center, J. Miranda Avenue, Concepcion Pequeña, Naga City.

Not all businesses require a Sanitary Permit. Only those handling food, providing personal care services, operating healthcare facilities, or accommodating the public overnight are required to secure one per PD 856.

Processing Time: 2 working days from submission of complete requirements.

Effective Date: 2015-07-01. Last amended by Ordinance No. 2022-089 (increased food service fee from PHP 200.00 to PHP 300.00, effective 2022-06-01).
